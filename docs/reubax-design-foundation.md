# REUBAX Design Foundation

## 1. Site architecture

- `/`: Homepage with positioning, trust, process, audience split, use cases, and conversion CTA
- `/how-it-works`: Process explanation and matching methodology
- `/for-businesses`: Buyer-facing value proposition and inquiry path
- `/for-partners`: Consulting partner value proposition and application path
- `/about`: Company model, role clarity, and brand principles
- `/contact`: Dual-path contact surface for both audiences

## 2. Design system

- Tone: premium, calm, credible, commercially serious
- Layout: generous whitespace, wide containers, sharp alignment, restrained card system
- Surfaces: layered neutral backgrounds, soft borders, subtle shadows, selective dark contrast blocks
- Components: sticky nav, hero panels, stat chips, information cards, CTA blocks, dual inquiry forms

## 3. Homepage wireframe

1. Hero with dual CTA and credibility chips
2. Audience overview panel showing demand side and supply side
3. Positioning band clarifying REUBAX as intermediary, not dev shop
4. Trust/model section
5. Three-step process section
6. Audience value split
7. Use cases
8. Proof placeholders
9. Conversion routes preview
10. Final CTA

## 4. Typography scale

- Display hero: `text-5xl` to `text-7xl`
- Section hero: `text-5xl` to `text-7xl`
- Section title: `text-3xl` to `text-5xl`
- Card title: `text-xl` to `text-2xl`
- Body: base to large with 1.75 to 2rem line rhythm
- Eyebrow labels: 11px to 12px uppercase with wide tracking

## 5. Color palette

- Background: warm off-white
- Foreground: deep slate
- Muted surfaces: soft neutral gray
- Primary accent: refined teal-cyan
- Supporting accent: muted gold
- Dark contrast surface: slate-950

## 6. Button styles

- Primary: teal fill, white text, elevated shadow, subtle hover lift
- Secondary: dark ink fill, premium utility action
- Outline: transparent light-on-dark CTA button
- Ghost: quiet nav/secondary utility button

## 7. Animation guidelines

- Use Framer Motion for short fade-up entrances
- Animate only to support hierarchy and scanning
- Keep movement small and durations controlled
- Stagger cards lightly, avoid decorative motion
- Premium pacing should feel deliberate, not playful
